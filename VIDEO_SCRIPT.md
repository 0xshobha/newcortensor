## RECORDING TIPS:

1. **Pre-record the demo** - don't do it live to avoid lag
2. **Use a clean browser** - no personal bookmarks/extensions visible
3. **Prepare the prompts** - have them ready to paste for smooth flow
4. **Test the evidence bundle** - make sure it has good data before recording
5. **Practice the voiceover** - conversational but confident tone
6. **Add captions** - many viewers watch without sound
